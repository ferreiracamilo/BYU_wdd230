Explore the Heart of Montevideo

Discover the vibrant soul of Montevideo as you explore the charming neighborhoods of Cordón, Centro, and Ciudad Vieja. Our association of stores invites you to wander through historic streets and modern avenues, where you'll find a delightful mix of boutiques, cafes, and artisans. Experience the essence of Montevideo's culture and shop at our local businesses that capture the city's unique spirit.

Shop Local, Feel Global

Support the heartbeat of Montevideo by shopping at our diverse array of stores in Cordón, Centro, and Ciudad Vieja. From trendy fashion boutiques to cozy bookshops and eclectic art galleries, our association offers a world of choices right in the heart of the city. By choosing local, you're not just buying products; you're investing in the culture and community of Montevideo.

Montevideo's Hidden Treasures

Unearth Montevideo's hidden gems in Cordón, Centro, and Ciudad Vieja. Our association of stores takes you on a journey through three unique districts, each with its own character and allure. Whether you're searching for vintage finds, gourmet delights, or handmade crafts, our members showcase the best that Montevideo has to offer. Explore, indulge, and experience the magic of our city through our local businesses.
